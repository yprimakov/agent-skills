Tax Optimization Notes

  • REIT position (O): Distributions are largely ordinary income (non-qualified). Consider holding O in a Roth IRA if available to eliminate tax drag on the 5.6% yield.
  • BDC (MAIN): Pass-through entity — distributions classified as ordinary income at 22%. High yield compensates, but tax-deferred accounts are preferred.
  • ENB (Canadian): Subject to 15% Canadian withholding tax in taxable accounts; recoverable as foreign tax credit on Form 1116.
  • Qualified dividend threshold: At 22% bracket, qualified dividends are taxed at 15%, not 0%. Reducing AGI below $94,050 (MFJ 2026) would move to 0% rate.
  • Tax-loss harvesting: MO and T are candidates for year-end loss harvesting if either falls 10%+ below cost basis.
